Wizard

Channel lightning from a magical book, reducing enemy dfense on the top path or hitting multiple enemies on the bottom path. Base tower (0-0) has hidden detection!

Cost: 6500 Tix

Requires: Level 10

This tower is an invaluable asset to any team thanks to its innate Hidden detection from the start, allowing it to be deployed confidently to handle sneaky enemies without prior preparation. Paired with its decent damage and good attack speed, it remains effective throughout the game. Both upgrade paths offer unique advantages, ensuring this tower can adapt to different strategies and fill essential roles in any composition.

Upgrades

The top path emphasizes increased DPS, attack speed, and range, all while being more affordable than its bottom path counterpart. Additionally, it offers a unique utility by piercing through damage reduction effects on enemies and applying a debuff that diminishes their damage reduction capabilities. This combination of offensive and supportive capabilities makes it slightly superior to the bottom path, as its debuffing ability provides significant value in challenging situations.


The bottom path trades some DPS, attack speed, range, and cost efficiency compared to the top path, but compensates with the ability to chain attacks to nearby enemies. This makes it highly effective against groups, with the chaining effect improving as it is upgraded, allowing it to outperform the top path in specific scenarios. However, its lower overall stats and lack of supportive abilities place it slightly below the top path in versatility and reliability.
