Heavy Gunner

Wielding a minigun with fast firerate! A powerful tower.

Cost: 10000 Tix

Requires: Level 20

This tower boasts the highest DPS in the game, delivering immense damage that rivals its steep cost. While it offers excellent range to support its powerful attacks, it also comes with significant drawbacks. The most notable is its incredibly expensive Hidden detection, which makes it hard to justify the investment for a typical round. Additionally, its overall cost makes it a difficult tower to fully leverage, as it requires substantial resources that could be better allocated elsewhere in most gameplay scenarios. While it excels in damage output, its price tag limits its practicality in many cases.

Upgrades

The top path grants this tower Hidden detection at its 4-X upgrade, along with the added bonus of all attacks dealing splash damage. This makes it the most effective and versatile upgrade in the game, capable of handling a variety of threats with both damage and coverage. However, its major downside is its extraordinarily high cost, making it the most expensive path in the game. This steep price tag makes it less desirable, as there are more cost-effective options available for Hidden detection and overall coverage, making the top path a less viable choice for most players seeking efficiency.


The bottom path has the cheapest upgrade for this tower, yet it remains the second most expensive upgrade in the game. Despite its high cost, it is unmatched in terms of DPS, making it an excellent choice for dealing raw damage in any situation, provided you have the resources to afford it. This path excels at delivering immense damage output, but its hefty price tag limits its use, requiring a substantial cash surplus to make it a worthwhile investment. For players looking to maximize DPS, this path is a top contender, though its cost may deter some.
